About me.

Hello again, in this is part you are going to know more about me. My name is Ali Nacir and I am from kenitra,Morocco. I am a high scool student who is interested in reading, computer programming, travelling and playing soccer. I am also interested in opportunities like camps and events.

As a lot of people, I did not know much about coding until I went to high school. Back then, I did know just the purpose of coding which means that I don'nt know even how a code looks like. When I got to high school, I decided to change the school. And that school that I got to have computer science class. Comparing to my other friends who has already started coding, I was not in the level to keeping out with my classmates. I decided to focus with the teacher as much as I can during the class. But my plan did not work out like I was expected.



The class of computer programming didn't exceed 2 hours a week. And,the teacher want us to learn in as much programs as possible. So we got to jump from a program to another before we got to learn all his aspects. At the end of the year, I forgot everything about computer programming. And the computer science class ends in the 10th, so it was my last chance for me to learn computer science at school and I was not in the level that I want to be.

I decided to start learning computer science by myself. First, I did not know which website I will use. So,I kept searching for the website where I can learn mora fastly. I sign up for a class in EDX called CS50 that I found very special and the teacher give the lessons in a way I did not get bored. I also started python in codeacademy which start step by step to make you learn the program that you want.



As I said I am very interested in opportunities. In that summer,while I was surfing on the net. I found a very interesting opportunity in Morocco. It was a computer science camp. In fact, it was the first computer science camp in Morocco. I was very excited because that camp will connect the dots that I had at school. And from 264 persons who applied. We were 24 who got accepted, 12 girls and 12 boys.

It was a great experience to be part of the first edition of e-ntitiate camp. We met other people who are interested in computer science and I got to know each and who got different levels in programming. In the camp we learnt python and Html. Special thanks to the monitors,the staff, the volunteers and escpecially the teachers who give their free time to help us.
